Onion Rings

Ingredients

  • 2 large sweet onions, sliced 1/2 inch thick

Batter:

  • ⅔ cup buttermilk
  • 1 egg
  • ¼ cup all-purpose flour
  • ½ teaspoon adobo all-purpose seasoning

Breading:

  • 2 cups panko bread crumbs
  • 1 teaspoon adobo all-purpose seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt

Steps

  1. Whisk the batter ingredients together in a shallow bowl. Cover and refrigerate it for 30 minutes
  2. Combine the breading ingredients together in a seperate shallow bowl
  3. Remove batter from fridge. Dip onion rings into the batter than into bread crumb mixture, turn to coat, and shake off excess crumbs
  4. Preheat air fryer to 170 degrees C. Line the air fryer with a parchment paper
  5. Place the breaded onion rings into air fryer leaving about 1/2-inch space between the slices
  6. Cook for about 10 to 12 minutes, flipping halfway through
  7. Remove from air fryer, transfer to a plate and sprinkle with kosher salt
