Lemonade

Ingredients

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 9 medium lemons
  • 7 cups ice cold water
  • ice

Steps

  1. Combine sugar and 1 cup of water in a small saucepan. Stir to dissolve sugar while mixture comes to a boil. Set aside to cool
  2. Cut the lemon into half and squeeze into a measuring cup. Discard any seeds. Continue to juice until you have 1 1/2 cups of fresh juice
  3. Pour 7 cups ice cold water into a pitcher. Stir in lemon juice and pulp, then add the syrup cooked to taste
